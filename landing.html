About Jungkook

Jeon Jung-kook (born September 1, 1997), known mononymously as Jungkook, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and record producer. He is the youngest member and vocalist of the South Korean boy band BTS.

Jungkook has been referred to as the "Golden Maknae" (golden youngest) of BTS because of his outstanding talents in singing, dancing, and even sports. He has released three solo songs as part of BTS' discography: "Begin", "Euphoria", and "My Time".
